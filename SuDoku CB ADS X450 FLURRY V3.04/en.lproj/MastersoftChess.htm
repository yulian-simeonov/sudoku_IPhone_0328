Welcome

Welcome to Mastersoft Chess!

www.MastersoftMobileSolutions.com

 

Mastersoft Chess is an easy to use yet powerful chess playing program for iPod Touch and iPhone. It allows you to play against your iPhone / iPod touch or a human opponent and lets you save and load games.

 

Mastersoft Chess features a very strong chess engine and plays interesting and sometimes quite aggressive chess! The engine is a successor of GromitChess, which finished 4th in the World Microcomputer Chess Championships 2001, winning the title of the 18th World Microcomputer Amateur Chess Champion.

Overview

Mastersoft Chess is very easy to use. Most of the commonly used commands can be accessed with the buttons on the toolbar. The buttons are (from left to right): take back move, show game moves, play next move, start thinking (move now), hint, flip board, options/level, new game.

The main screen shows a full-size board, above the board you see information about the search-process and the chess clock.

Moves can be entered by either dragging the piece with your fingertip from it's current position to the destination square or by tapping the piece and the destination square. Selected squares have a highlit rectangle to provide visible feedback. Enter the kingmove to castle and the pawnmove to make an en passant capture or promotion. Mastersoft Chess prevents unintentional selection of squares by only allowing squares to be selected if there is a legal move to or from that square. The standard rules of chess apply.

When Mastersoft Chess is started, it loads the state of the game last played.

New game

Starts a new game from the initial position and with the currently selected level. If you want to play black just press the Move Now button in the toolbar and Mastersoft Chess will make the first move.

Game moves

Shows header-information and the complete moves history of the current game. The current move is highlighted. When you tap a different move it will be highlighted and the position after the selected move becomes the current position.
There are three checkboxes Info, Comments and Lines which let you select whether you want to see the header, comments or lines (variations). You can for example disable comments and lines if you have a heavily commented and analyzed game with many variations and only want to see the moves.

Promotion

When you move a pawn to the first/eight rank you can select which piece (queen, rook, bishop, knight) to promote to.

Load game

Mastersoft Chess loads games from your iPod Touch / iPhone in standard PGN format (Portable Game Notation).
Load game opens a file selection-dialog where you can select the filename of your saved game (.pgn-file).

Please note that at this time there is no way to transfer saved games from your iPod Touch / iPhone to other devices eg a PC. The load game facility is for loading your own previously stored games.

Save game

Mastersoft Chess saves games to your iPod Touch / iPhone in standard PGN format (Portable Game Notation).
Save game allows you to save the current game in a (.pgn) file. You can modify names of the players, the site, the event, the result and so on.

Please note that at this time there is no way to transfer saved games from your iPod Touch / iPhone to other devices eg a PC. The save game facility is there so that you can re-load your current game at a later time.

Options

The options-dialog lets you select the playing level and configure the board.

  • Level
    Select between 11 predefined levels:
    • Novice: easiest level of play
    • Beginner: slightly stronger than Novice
    • Advanced: tactically stronger than Beginner
    • 1sec/move: average time 1 second per move
    • 2sec/move: average time 2 second per move
    • 5sec/move: average time 5 second per move
    • 10sec/move: average time 10 second per move
    • 15sec/move: average time 15 second per move
    • 30sec/move: average time 30 second per move
    • 1min/move: average time 1 minute per move
    • infinite: computer is thinking till maximum searchdepth is reached or till a mate is found. You can stop the thinking process by Move now.
  • Pieceset
    Select between several different styles of chessmen.
  • Square colors
    Select between several different board styles.
  • Animation
    Select speed of animation when pieces move on the board
    • off:
      • pieces don't slide but jump.
    • slow:
      • slow sliding, same speed as in Hint.
    • medium:
      • normal speed.
    • fast:
      • fast sliding.
  • Against iPhone
    If checked Mastersoft Chess will start thinking after each move you make, otherwise you can make moves for both sides (2-player-mode).
  • Highlight last move
    Tells Mastersoft Chess to highlight the last move made by the engine.
  • Show mainline
    Uncheck if you don't want to see search related information when the engine is running.
  • Sound effects
    Uncheck to switch off sound effects.

Levels

Mastersoft Chess provides various levels which can be selected in the options dialog. To reduce Mastersoft Chess's strength you can choose Novice. Note that Mastersoft Chess is suitable for absolute beginners right up to World competition level.

Move now

Forces Mastersoft Chess to make the next move, for example when you want to switch sides.

Flip board

Turns the chessboard around. To let Mastersoft Chess make the next move select Move now.

Take back

Take back one move. Press twice to take back your move and Mastersoft Chess's reply.

Move forward

Allows you to replay moves after taking them back.

Setup board
  • To add a piece select it from the white/black pieces on the bottom, and tap the desired square.
  • To clear a square tap it.
  • There are buttons to clear the board, set up the initial position and undo all changes made so far.
After setting up the pieces and selecting DONE you can set the player on move, the castling, and the enpassant status in the next window. You can't close the window until the position is legal. For example it is not legal if:
  • there is not exactly 1 king of each color
  • there are more than 8 pawns, 9 queens or 10 other pieces of a given color
  • castling and enpassant status do not match the given position

Hint

Lets Mastersoft Chess suggest a good move for you. The engine performs a brief search (of about 5 seconds) and suggests a move by slowly doing/undoing the hint-move on the board.

 

Answers to your feedback

1. To play black, simply start a new game then press the move now icon on the bottom bar.

2. If the computer's pawn appears to make an unusual move to take your own pawn, check out the "En Passant" chess rule. This is a standard and important chess rule that most chess games fail to implement. Learn it now so that you can master it and use it to your advantage!

3. You cannot castle if any of the following are true:

  1. Your king is in check.
  2. Your king has been moved .
  3. The rook that you wish to castle with has been moved .
  4. Your king would be in check after castling.
  5. There are pieces inbetween your king and the rook that you wish to castle with.
  6. Your king passes through a square that is being attacked by your opponent.
